CIE XYZ Color Matching Functions.
================================================================

lin2012xyz2e_fine_7sf.csv:
2-deg XYZ CMFs transformed from the CIE (2006) 2-deg LMS cone fundamentals.
http://www.cvrl.org/database/text/cienewxyz/cie2012xyz2.htm

lin2012xyz10e_fine_7sf.csv:
10-deg XYZ CMFs transformed from the CIE (2006) 10-deg LMS cone fundamentals.
http://www.cvrl.org/database/text/cienewxyz/cie2012xyz10.htm

ciexyz31_1.csv

http://www.cvrl.org/database/text/cmfs/ciexyz31.htm

http://www.cvrl.org/database/text/cmfs/ciexyzj.htm

ciexyzjv.csv

http://www.cvrl.org/database/text/cmfs/ciexyzjv.htm

ciexyz64_1.csv
CIE 1964 10-deg, XYZ CMFs.
http://www.cvrl.org/database/text/cmfs/ciexyz64.htm
